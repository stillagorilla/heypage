Project Manager

San Francisco

Apply

Under the direction of others, designs, architects and evaluates company products that provide elegant solutions for end users. Assist in providing product usability, evaluation and support to product development teams, including the analysis and investigation of applications/systems, graphics, web, multimedia, voice response and conversational user interfaces.

Roles and Responsibilities:

Show more
  • The User Experience Designer will be working within the Web of Science division of Clarivate Analytics, on existing and new web-based applications intended to meet the needs of professionals working in the drug research and discovery phase of drug development.
  • The User Experience Designer is responsible for meeting design objectives of individual projects (e.g. user research, delivering user interface designs to specification, validating usability with testing, etc) but also to support the strategic goals to increase the awareness and impact of User Experience (UX) activities across the organisation’s range of products.
  • Take responsibility for the user interface (UI) design of individual projects within the Web of Science portfolio, throughout the entire product lifecycle, from initial ideation and conceptualisation through to final design deliverable.
  • Work collaboratively and actively with project teams to develop UI designs for projects.

Technical / Professional Skills & Competencies:

  • UX expertise including hands-on proficiency in problem solving, conceptualization and analysis, wireframe design, information architecture, expert reviews/heuristic evaluation, collaborative design.
  • Proactively support other team members to share team workload.
  • Peer reviews, able to give and receive constructive feedback on all UX deliverables.
